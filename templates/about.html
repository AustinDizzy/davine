What is this?

Daine is a social analytics service built for Vine. In English speak, we can track how much your vines are growing by and show them to you in a few pretty graphs and tables. In Technology speak, we're an Open Data service that crawls Vine for users to track daily metrics for. We're fully open source, open data, and ad-free.

Who is this?

I'll tell you one legal entity we aren't, Vine. We are in no way affiliad with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Vine Labs, Inc or Twitter Inc. We're just one college kid and a noble investor, both trying to make something useful out of our time and skills.

F.A.Q.

What all do you track? - As of writing, we track the following for each Vine member: number of followers; number of people user is following; total number of loops; number of revines; number of original posts; changes (including modification date) of username, profile location, profile background color, profile description; if and when a user becomes verified; and the deltas of all numeric values.

What profiles do you track analytics for? - Any profiles queried through our homepage search are auomatically added to our queue and their metrics will be fetch each day at 12AM Eastern Standard Time. Additionally, any users who reach the front two pages of the popular page or editors pick will automatically be added to the queue.

You say you're open source and open data. Where can I access the source and all data? - The Davine project is FOSS licensed under the MIT Open Source License. You can access the source, and report any issues, on its Github page. You can download the data for an individual user on their profile page here on Davine. Simply click the " Download" button when on a user's page.

I do not want Davine to track my profile's metrics. How can I prevent Davine from doing so? - In order to prevent Davine, and any other third-party service, from scraping your publicly available Vine information, your profile and posts must be set to Private. Read the Vine FAQ page to learn how to do this.

I want to bulk/batch export specific users' data or bulk/batch export users' data from a search query. - Please, contact us at data@davine.co.
